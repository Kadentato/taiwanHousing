Describe a home — where it is, how big, and its features — and the model estimates what it would sell for today, as a single best guess plus a range. The estimate runs entirely in your browser from a gradient-boosted model trained on ~3 million sales (2012–2024) and cross-validated out-of-time; the ranges are calibrated so that, e.g., 4 in 5 real sales land inside the 80% band.

The home

"Unit's floor" = which storey the apartment is on; "total floors" = the building's height. For a standalone house (透天厝) you own every storey, so only the total applies.
